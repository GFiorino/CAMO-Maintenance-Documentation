MRX Blue Eye - Daily Workflow

This guide outlines the typical daily workflow for a CAMO Technical Administrator using the MRX Blue Eye maintenance tracking system.

Daily Routine

  • 📥 Log into MRX Blue Eye at the start of the day.
  • 📋 Review dashboard notifications:
    • Upcoming maintenance tasks (due dates approaching)
    • Pending Airworthiness Directives (ADs)
    • Open Service Bulletins (SBs) needing attention
  • 🔍 Check maintenance status of all active aircraft:
    • Update task status (Completed, Scheduled, Pending)
    • Verify compliance entries are correctly logged
  • 📑 Upload and file any new maintenance documents received:
    • Completed Work Orders
    • AD/SB Compliance Certificates
    • AMP Revision Documents
  • 📆 Coordinate with Part-145 maintenance providers:
    • Confirm scheduling of upcoming maintenance events
    • Ensure Work Orders are correctly linked to tasks
  • 🛡️ Support the Safety Management System (SMS):
    • Report any discrepancies, missing records, or documentation errors
  • 📬 Respond to technical queries and internal CAMO requests.
  • 🧹 End of day:
    • Verify that all updates are properly saved
    • Log out securely from MRX Blue Eye
