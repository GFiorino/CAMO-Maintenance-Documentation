MRX Blue Eye - Daily Workflow

This checklist outlines the practical daily routine for a CAMO Technical Administrator using the MRX Blue Eye maintenance tracking system.

Daily Checklist

  • ☑️ Log into MRX Blue Eye.
  • ☑️ Review dashboard notifications:
    • ☑️ Check upcoming maintenance tasks (due dates).
    • ☑️ Review pending Airworthiness Directives (ADs).
    • ☑️ Review open Service Bulletins (SBs).
  • ☑️ Check maintenance status of all active aircraft:
    • ☑️ Update task statuses (Completed / Scheduled / Pending).
    • ☑️ Verify compliance entries are correctly logged.
  • ☑️ Upload and file new maintenance documents:
    • ☑️ Completed Work Orders.
    • ☑️ AD/SB Compliance Certificates.
    • ☑️ AMP Revision Updates.
  • ☑️ Coordinate with Part-145 maintenance providers:
    • ☑️ Confirm scheduling of upcoming maintenance.
    • ☑️ Ensure Work Orders are correctly linked.
  • ☑️ Support the Safety Management System (SMS):
    • ☑️ Report any discrepancies or missing records.
  • ☑️ Respond to technical queries and internal CAMO requests.
  • ☑️ End of day:
    • ☑️ Verify all updates are saved correctly.
    • ☑️ Log out securely from MRX Blue Eye.
