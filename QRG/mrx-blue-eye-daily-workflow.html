MRX Blue Eye – Daily Workflow

This checklist outlines the typical daily routine for a CAMO Technical Administrator using the MRX Blue Eye maintenance tracking system to manage maintenance records and compliance.

🖥 1. System Login and Dashboard Review

  • ☐ Log into MRX Blue Eye securely
  • ☐ Review dashboard notifications for:
    • ☐ Upcoming maintenance tasks
    • ☐ Pending Airworthiness Directives (ADs)
    • ☐ Open Service Bulletins (SBs)

🔍 2. Maintenance Tracking Updates

  • ☐ Review maintenance status of all active aircraft
  • ☐ Update task statuses (Completed / Scheduled / Pending)
  • ☐ Verify that compliance entries are accurate and complete

📑 3. Document Management

  • ☐ Upload and file completed Work Orders
  • ☐ Upload AD/SB Compliance Certificates
  • ☐ Upload AMP Revision Documents

📆 4. Maintenance Coordination

  • ☐ Confirm scheduling of upcoming maintenance events with Part-145 providers
  • ☐ Ensure Work Orders are correctly linked to tasks

🛡️ 5. Safety Management System (SMS) Support

  • ☐ Report discrepancies, missing records, or documentation errors

📨 6. Communication and Follow-Up

  • ☐ Respond to technical queries and internal CAMO requests

🧹 7. End of Day Checks

  • ☐ Verify that all updates are properly saved
  • ☐ Log out securely from MRX Blue Eye
