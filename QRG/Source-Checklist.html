CAMO Admin Daily QRG – Source Checklist

This checklist helps CAMO Administrators maintain full compliance and data accuracy by tracking inputs based on their origin. Ideal for daily operations, audits, and process standardization.

✉️ 1. From Authorities (EASA, FAA, State of Design)

  • ☑ Have I checked for new Airworthiness Directives (ADs)?
  • ☑ Have I entered relevant ADs into the Maintenance Tracking System?
  • ☑ Are deadlines and applicability details accurate and up to date?

🏢 2. From Manufacturers (OEMs)

  • ☑ Have I reviewed the latest Service Bulletins (SBs)?
  • ☑ Have I assessed SB applicability to our aircraft configuration?
  • ☑ If the SB includes a modification, has it been recorded?
  • ☑ Is the SB repetitive or mandatory, and does it require inclusion in the AMP?

📃 3. From Aircraft Technical Logbook

  • ☑ Have I logged all new defects from Tech Logs or PIREPs?
  • ☑ Are MEL/CDL-deferred items tracked and categorized?
  • ☑ Is each defect assigned a clear status: Open / Deferred / Closed?

📊 4. From Flight Ops / Usage Data

  • ☑ Are flight hours and cycles up to date in the system?
  • ☑ Have I checked for triggered AMP tasks or LLP limits?
  • ☑ Are Life-Limited Part (LLP) counters and deadlines accurate?

🔧 5. From Line/Base Maintenance

  • ☑ Have I recorded all component removals/installations?
  • ☑ Is all traceability information (who/what/when/why) archived?
  • ☑ Have I updated AMP compliance based on completed work?

📄 6. From Internal CAMO Operations

  • ☑ Is the AMP current and reflecting regulatory updates?
  • ☑ Have I created or updated Work Orders / Work Packages?
  • ☑ Are completed tasks logged with a valid CRS (Certificate of Release to Service)?
  • ☑ Have I prepared supporting documents for ARC renewal or upcoming audits?
